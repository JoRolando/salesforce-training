
# Week 1 - Day 1 CRM Basics

## What is CRM?

CRM (Customer Relationship Management) is a system used to manage customer interactions, sales, support, and communication.

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce

- Manage customer data
- Automate workflows
- Improve productivity
- Track sales and support

---

## Salesforce Concepts

### Account
Represents a company or organization.

### Contact
Represents an individual person.

### Opportunity
Represents a potential deal or business opportunity.

---

## Real-World Mapping (College System)

| Salesforce Concept | Example |
|---|---|
| Account | College |
| Contact | Student |
| Lead | Student Applicant |
| Opportunity | Admission Process |

---

## Business Flow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer
